Credit Rating Score Generation

1. Credit Rating Score Overview

Credit rating scores are used to assess the creditworthiness of customers based on their financial behavior and history. These scores help in making informed decisions about lending, risk management, and customer segmentation. Microsoft Fabric provides tools to calculate and analyze credit rating scores efficiently.

2. Scoring Models

Scoring models are used to calculate credit rating scores based on predefined conditions and rules. These models incorporate factors like payment history, outstanding debt, and credit utilization. Microsoft Fabric's integration with Python and Power BI allows for the creation and customization of scoring models.

3. Reporting and Insights

Once credit rating scores are generated, they can be visualized and analyzed using Power BI dashboards. These reports provide actionable insights for risk assessment, customer segmentation, and decision-making. Microsoft Fabric's integration with Power BI ensures real-time and interactive reporting.
